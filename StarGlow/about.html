Our Philosophy

Our School is committed to placing the best interest of children welfare, care and development at the center of all staff matters.

Our Aim

To provide a safe and stimulating environment in which children feel happy and secure.

Encourage the emotional,personal,social,physical,creative and intellectual development of the children.

Encourage positive towards self and others and develope confidence and self esteem.
