Career

"We can accomplish almost anything within our ability, if we think we can!" Opportunities to develop a rewarding, satisfying professional life and career are abundant at Mansai Inc. Those who join us are joining a leader in IT consulting. The huge diversity of opportunities within Mansai Inc allows for career planning options to suit the variety of needs, skills and interests among our employees. We strive to provide flexible career paths and options. Employees interested in global positions get an exposure to the numerous opportunities created by our network, while those seeking assignments in USA find rewarding career options readily available.

We are providing abundant learning opportunities for our employees, creating a healthy competitive work environment. Our employees experience the exhilaration of working across the width and breadth of our business and share in the wealth created through our success. Most of the careers follow the work and professional development path that is set out below: Programmer Analysts develop core skills in a technology/service area. When they have developed the necessary experience, they begin to supervise and train others.

Consultants begin to focus on building leadership skills. At this stage, they also develop a solid foundation in applying core competency and problem-analytical skills. Senior consultants broaden their team management skills, while concentrating on a specific service, industry or other specialized area.

Managers are people with an extremely specialized skill set. In this role they can exhibit their leadership abilities and exercise these skills at the client and/or on internal projects. Mansai Inc rewards the creme-de-la-creme by making them partners in our business. Partners always supervise existing client relationships and forge new and enduring affinity. They also exercise independent judgment in business development.

Mansai Inc's career development programs are created to steer every employee's career towards a satisfying job in the information technology industry, while mapping a path for career growth. These programs also add technical proficiency to the employee's communication, problem solving and leadership skills thus, enhancing their overall profile Mansai Inc makes a large investment in training and development, which works out to an average of about 600 hours of training per professional during their first five years.

Mansai Inc also offers mentorship and higher education to all its employees as a part of in-house Career Development Programs. Employees are also encouraged to attend Seminars/Conferences to keep them abreast of every advancement in their field of interest. Throughout their careers, employees build their skills and expand their horizons, and have opportunities to immerse in and affiliate themselves with specific industries and broad service areas.
