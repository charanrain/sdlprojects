Employee Data Bank

The Employee Data Bank Module maintains all relevant employee related information, including different types of personal information, detailed qualifications and work experience, job related information etc. Information captured in this module is utilized by all other modules, thus eliminating data redundancy..


Employee self Service

The Employee Self Service Portal is a powerful tool providing employees the ability to view and update personal information via the web without having to go through the HR staff. The functionality of this module spans through the entire system, making information available anywhere, anytime. All information is subject to company defined security policy, allowing the employee to view only the information he / she is authorized to view..


Attendance Management

The Time & Attendance Module automates time tracking related processes and enhances the organization's performance by eliminating paperwork and manual processes associated with time and attendance needs. The sophisticated module helps to efficiently organize labor data, improve the workforce management and minimize errors in enforcement of company's attendance policies..


Leave Managment System

Leave Management is a workflow-based system that automates the leave application and approval process. This system enables users to apply for leave online and to view the status of the application as well as the complete information of the leave balances. Leave applications are submitted to their respective authorities for approval. .


Payroll Management

SmartHR Payroll is specially designed and developed for INDIAN and global organization, is Web based applications and can be integrated with attendance system. Supports pay slip, IT declarations, YTD statement, IT statement, Tax Deduction at Source (TDS), Employee investment under various sections of Income Tax. High flexibility in defining various allowances, deductions, leave rules etc. for the employees, Highly flexible to define PT, IT slabs, bonus, loans, increments etc.


Training Management

SmartHR Training Management software provides complete automation of training activities, customizable to client’s need.


Performance Management

SmartHR – Performance Management software provides complete automation of Employees Performance Appraisal activities with a scope to undertake client’s customization to suit design and uniqueness appraisal system of organization..


Recruitment

Smart HR - Recruitment & Applicant Module provides a comprehensive solution for the entire recruitment process, including requests for staff, approval of vacancies, entering requirements, capturing candidates information, short-listing, interview notes and other features. This module also allows HR professionals to generate templates and documentation to streamline the recruitment process..


Appraisal

A workflow integrated module which helps to initiate the process, view status and set alerts for completion. You can also view previous records and track employee performance details. .


Asset Managment

Keep track of all company owned assets which have been assigned to employees. Get details what items, when and to whom it was assigned..


Travel Management

SDL Travel Management module, enables you to manage requests for travel with ease and flexibility. Our travel module seamlessly integrates commercial, chartered and self-operated travel on any mode of transportation - air, bus, train, and so on. A simple and elegant web interface makes the travel request and booking process easy. All users have access to book their travel in a few clicks. .


Dashboard Managment

The dashboard provides you with a snap shot of various details in graphical format. Each user role will have the customized dashboard showing various information pertaining to the particular role. A very user friendly interface with real time information and pending tasks and notifications..
