Support Center

  2. Support Center

SmartHR offers an elegant, intuitive interface with easy navigation and excellent support. To help you get started, you can choose from a number of training tools and options ranging from facilitated demonstrations to customer onboarding sessions.


Training

Context-sensitive video tutorials:

When you log into your new SmartHR account, you will want to get started right away. To help make that easy, we’ve embedded a number of context-sensitive videos that explain the most common administrative activities.

Supplementary tutorial video library:

Additional tutorial videos are just a click away in our support library. These videos will walk you through the various areas of SmartHR and show you exactly how everything works.

Administrator remote training:

If you prefer to have someone answer questions as you setup and familiarize yourself with SmartHR, one of our customer success specialists will provide the remote shared screen training you need to get comfortable with all aspects of the software.

Employee/Manager remote training:

Remote training sessions can also be arranged for employees and managers. Different sessions are typically held for employees and managers so that the trainer can focus on what is most important to each group.

On-site group training:

In the interests of limiting our impact on the environment, we strive to meet most training needs remotely; however, on-site training can be provided if required. Please contact us for more information about on-site training options. Review the services page for more information about included and additional implementation services provided by SmartHR.

Services

Successfully implementing your new HRIS

We are committed to helping you successfully implement SmartHR. That’s why we call our support staff “customer success specialists.” Even the easiest, most exciting new technologies need an effective implementation approach to ensure buy-in and uptake across the company. We’re here for you every step of the way if you need us, or we can point you to our extensive online resources if you’d rather implement on your own. Regardless of your needs, we’ll tailor our approach to assist you and your organization.
You have invested a lot of time and energy into choosing the right HR software for your organization. Let us help you communicate the excitement of this important step forward, and help make your SmartHR implementation a huge success!

Help Desk

SmartHR support community and help desk

When you login to the SmartHR support community and help desk, you gain immediate access to the cumulative knowledge of thousands of SmartHR users, as well as the SmartHR technical support team.
You might ask the user community how they implemented SmartHR to maximize uptake, or read about the many creative ways social HR is being used to increase employee engagement.
You can use the help desk to submit your own support requests to our trained technical support professionals. Every request is assigned to a support team member and tracked through to a satisfactory resolution. The help desk is also your channel for sharing feedback about SmartHR or submitting your ideas for new features and product improvements. We love feedback!

Others ways to follow or access SmartHR support

If you have a technical bent and want to plug in to SmartHR’s development team for the latest information about recent and planned upgrades, check out http://developers.SmartHR.com
To stay on top of SmartHR news, events and blog posts, follow us on Twitter: @SmartHR
You can also contact the support team directly at support@SmartHR.com
