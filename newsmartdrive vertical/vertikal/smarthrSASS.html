SmartHR is a revolutionary system that promises to take the pain out of HR Management. SmartHR - Smartly manages , Employee Databank, Employee Self Service, Attendance, Leave, payroll, Recruitment, Training, Performance Appraisal and all such HRMS activities from candidate entry till employee exit.


What is SaaS?

SaaS, a Software as a Service provider is a company that offers individuals or enterprises access over the Internet to applications and related services that would otherwise have to be located in their own personal or enterprise computers. SaaS solutions are expected to become an important alternative, not only for smaller companies with low budgets for information technology, but also for larger companies as a form of outsourcing and for many services for individuals as well.

What advantages do SaaS solutions offer over traditional approaches?

Integrated flow from Recruitment to F&F, for advanced business needs. This provides strong business returns and outstanding value for money over the time of the application.
  • Cost reduction for technology. For users of all sizes SaaS advantage is a monthly fee to access applications instead of having to invest thousands or millions to create and maintain IT infrastructure.
  • Shorter implementation cycles compared to a typical in-house rollout of technology.
  • The company can focus on its "core competency".
  • Eliminates or reduces recruiting, retaining and retraining IT personnel.

What are the short-term benefits?

  • Enables users to access state-of-the-art technology immediately without upfront investment in hardware and software.
  • Implementation is usually significantly faster than internal solutions.
  • Accelerates time-to-benefit

What are the long-term benefits?

  • Software upgrades at no additional cost avoids technology obsolescence.
  • No retraining of IT personnel as technologies change.
  • More available capital to fund business growth.
  • Scalability as you grow.

Why SaaS?

Zero Capital Expenditure

Zero capital expense. No hardware to buy, No Software license to purchase, No databases to procure.

No maintenance

No data backups to keep, No database to administer, No autoupdates, No virus scans, No security drills, No performance tuning, No DR/BCP planning.

No software upgrades

Automatic updates to latest version - always. Automatic changes based on statutory regulations.

Significantly lower cost

Fixed monthly subscription fee, no AMC, higher ROI.

Quick start

Get up and running in a matter of hours and not in weeks and months!

Anytime anywhere access

All you need is a web browser. Access from home, office, hotel or coffee shop or just about wherever you need - whenever you need!

How does it work?

Start:

SmartHR is complete HR and Payroll software hosted on the Internet. You do not need to purchase the license or buy the servers to use it. You do not even need to download anything or install any additional software on your PC. All you need is a web browser. All the users of your HR department, your payroll and in-fact your entire staff could be using it.

Super fast Implementation:

SmartHR comes with pre-defined implementation templates. As soon as your account is created, you are ready to login on the same day ! The pre-built templates and sample data enables you to start using the system immediately without going in for an elaborate training or system configuration. You can change the default setup configurations as per your requirements.

Training:

We have made it really simple to get up and running with SmartHR - HR Software on-demand. You don't need any special training to get up and running. In case you do need our help - we are just a click away. For paid editions, we provide free training and setup assistance.

Support:

Starter / Advance / Enterprise edition customers get and premium phone and email support. Freedom edition users can take advantage of Freedom support forum - managed by our helpdesk team members. Freedom edition users can also avail Setup assistance or premium support as an add-on option. You do not require any internal IT support to manage your server hardware, technology or updates. In addition, application level support is available to you directly from the experts !
